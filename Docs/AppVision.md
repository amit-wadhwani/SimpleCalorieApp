## ğŸ§  User Personas

### 1. The Conscious Tracker  
**Goal:** Stay aware of daily intake without obsessing over details.  
**Pain Points:** Overwhelmed by MyFitnessPal; wants quick logging.  
**Need:** Simplicity + honest calorie overview.

### 2. The Goal-Oriented Improver  
**Goal:** Lose or maintain weight intelligently.  
**Pain Points:** Confusing apps with inconsistent projections.  
**Need:** Reliable daily feedback + projections they can trust.

### 3. The Rational Optimizer  
**Goal:** Make decisions based on real data (Objectivist mindset).  
**Pain Points:** Manipulative design, gimmicky motivation systems.  
**Need:** Calm, data-driven feedback that respects reason and autonomy.
